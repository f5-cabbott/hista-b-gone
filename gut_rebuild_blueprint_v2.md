# Gut Architecture & Stabilization Blueprint

This operational master plan details the protocols required to rebuild gut architecture and stabilize a hyper-reactive immune profile. The objective is to shift from passive symptom mitigation to proactive structural tissue engineering.

---

## Phase 1: Defuse & Clear
**Operational Focus:** Drop the ambient histamine and biogenic amine payload to zero while manually clearing the gut lumen. This provides an immediate operational vacation for remaining, exhausted Diamine Oxidase (DAO) enzymes.

### The Supplement Stack
* **Exogenous DAO Enzyme:** *NaturDAO* (preferred for high activity units) or *Seeking Health Histamine Digest*. Protocol: 1 capsule ingested 20 minutes prior to every meal.
* **H1 + H2 Receptor Antagonists:** Standard *Cetirizine* (Zyrtec) or *Fexofenadine* (Allegra) coupled with *Famotidine* (Pepcid). 
  * *Note: These compounds do not clear histamine; they physically occupy the receptors to prevent vascular and neurological redlining while the DAO clearances take effect.*

### Diet Requirements
* **The Baseline:** Strictly fresh-cooked or frozen-at-sea animal proteins (chicken breast, ground pork, white fish thaw-and-sear).
* **The Prohibitions:** Absolute ban on leftovers. If cooked protein sits refrigerated for more than two hours, microbial conversion of histidine to histamine begins. Avoid all canned foods, fermented products, slow-cooked meats, and bone broths.

### Phase Timeline
* **Duration:** 14 Days.

## Phase 2: Quench & Stabilize
**Operational Focus:** Once external amine influx is controlled, target and lock down the hyper-reactive mast cells lining the GI tract and skin tissue to prevent spontaneous degranulation and localized inflammatory cascades.

### The Supplement Stack
* **Quercetin Phytosome:** *Thorne Quercetin Phytosome* (lipid-bound form to maximize systemic bioavailability). Protocol: 500mg taken 30 minutes before breakfast and dinner on an empty stomach.
* **Luteolin:** Bioflavonoid working synergistically with Quercetin to block mast-cell cytokine release. Protocol: 100mg daily.
* **Buffered Vitamin C:** Pure *Calcium Ascorbate* or *Magnesium Ascorbate* (avoid standard ascorbic acid to prevent mucosal irritation). Acts as a direct degradation catalyst for circulating histamine.

### Diet Requirements
* **The Baseline:** Incorporate mast-cell stabilizing vegetables and clean complex carbohydrates: sprouted wild rice, russet potatoes, asparagus, broccoli, and fresh-cut onions (high in natural quercetin).
* **The Prohibitions:** Maintain the Phase 1 ban on aged/fermented items. Add a strict exclusion of high-histamine or histamine-liberating plants: avocados, tomatoes, spinach, and citrus fruits.

### Phase Timeline
* **Duration:** 21 Days (overlapping with the transition out of Phase 1).

## Phase 3: Rebuild & Scale
**Operational Focus:** Suppress systemic tissue inflammation to allow enterocytes to divide and regrow the microvilli brush border, restoring native DAO enzyme synthesis.

### The Supplement Stack
* **Pure L-Glutamine Powder:** *Thorne* or *Protocol For Life Balance*. Protocol: 5 grams dissolved in room-temperature water first thing in the morning and immediately before bed. Scale up to 10 grams per dose if tolerated. This acts as direct cellular fuel for enterocyte remodeling.
* **Zinc Carnosine:** *PepZin GI* (Zinlori). Protocol: 75mg twice daily with meals. This specific chelate delivers zinc directly to the mucosal epithelial layer to repair tight junctions.
* **Serum-Derived Bovine Immunoglobulins (SBI):** *MegaIgG2000* or *Seeking Health IgG*. These function as a biological sponge, binding to residual bacterial toxins, lipopolysaccharides (LPS), and microbial remnants from the initial pathogen vector to prevent immune activation.

### Diet Requirements
* **The Baseline:** Solidify low-histamine maintenance. Introduce clean, unoxidized fats: ghee, premium extra virgin olive oil, raw pine nuts, and macadamia nut butter.
* **The Prohibitions:** Continued exclusion of high-risk items (citrus, vinegars, soy, walnuts, aged cheeses) until microvilli integrity is verified by complete symptom clearance.

### Phase Timeline
* **Duration:** 30 to 60 Days. Structural remodeling of a flattened brush border requires sustained cellular turnover.

## Appendix: Skincare & Summer SPF Protocol

Because the skin is highly dense with mast cells, internal gastrointestinal barrier failure frequently presents externally as flushing, hives, or dermatographia. Summer heat and UV exposure compound this via direct localized mast cell degranulation.

### Skincare Blacklist (Avoid Immediately)
* **Chemical UV Filters:** *Oxybenzone, Avobenzone, Octinoxate, and PABA*. These absorb UV light and convert it to heat via a chemical reaction on the skin. Localized thermal spikes and degradation byproducts are direct triggers for dermal mast cells.
* **Fermented Actives:** Avoid products containing *Bifida Ferment Lysate, Galactomyces*, or any probiotic/fermented extracts.
* **Preservatives & Stabilizers:** *Parabens, Benzoates (Sodium Benzoate), and Sulfites*.
* **Fragrances & Botanical Extracts:** "Parfum", essential oils, cinnamaldehyde, and Balsam of Peru (high-potency contact allergens causing immediate localized histamine release).

### Skincare Gold Standard (Gravitate Towards)
* **Pure Mineral Blockers:** Sunscreen must exclusively utilize **Non-Nano Zinc Oxide** or **Titanium Dioxide**. Zinc oxide is an inert physical barrier that deflects UV rays without generating heat or reacting with tissue, serving as a topical anti-inflammatory.
* **Barrier Structural Assets:** *Ceramides (1, 3, 6-II)* and *Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)* to repair the lipid rafts of the stratum corneum.
* **Calming Agents:** *Centella Asiatica (Cica)* and *Pure Aloe Vera Gel* (devoid of added green dyes or triethanolamine).

### Summer SPF Guidance
* **The Play:** Avoid standard drugstore chemical sunscreens to prevent immediate facial and neck flushing. Utilize premium, minimalist mineral formulas designed for hyper-reactive or post-procedure skin, such as **EltaMD UV Physical (Mineral Only)** or **Badger Damascus Rose Mineral Face Sunscreen** (fragrance-free variant). 
* **The Routine:** Apply generously before UV exposure, and rinse completely with a gentle, non-soap cleanser as soon as returning indoors to minimize physical occlusion stress on the skin barrier.
