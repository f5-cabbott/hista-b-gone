# Low-Histamine Grocery Procurement Guide: Metropolitan Market (West Seattle)

This checklist organizes low-histamine and amine-safe grocery selections following the physical perimeter horseshoe of the West Seattle Metropolitan Market. This list bypasses all canned goods, aged items, and industrial additives to protect the gut barrier.

---

## 1. Produce Department
*Focus on high-potency mast cell stabilizers, fresh aromatic bases, and clean complex carbohydrates.*

*   **Fresh Basil:** High-potency natural antihistamine and mast cell stabilizer that forms the herbal core of the alternative spread .
*   **Fresh Arugula:** Delivers a sharp, peppery mustard-like bite to substitute for black pepper without irritating the gut lining .
*   **Fresh Red & Yellow Onions:** Exceptionally rich in natural Quercetin, acting as a baseline dietary mast cell membrane stabilizer .
*   **Broccoli & Asparagus:** Low-histamine, high-fiber vegetable matrices that provide safe micronutrients without triggering systemic flares .
*   **Russet & Red Potatoes:** Clean, well-tolerated complex carbohydrates providing caloric density and a texturally satisfying chew when roasted .
*   **Fresh Garlic Bulbs:** Raw allicin delivers potent antimicrobial properties and an intense, savory flavor profile .

---

## 2. Condiments & Specialty Oils Aisle
*Procure pure, unoxidized fats and low-amine molecular flavor switches.*

*   **Premium Extra Virgin Olive Oil:** Cold-pressed monounsaturated fat that functions as an anti-inflammatory baseline liquid asset .
*   **Ghee (Clarified Butter):** Pure butter fat with casein and lactose entirely removed; highly stable for high-heat pan work.
*   **Green or White Verjuice:** Unfermented juice of unripe wine grapes; provides necessary sour acidity with zero histamine or amine load .
*   **Maldon Flaky Sea Salt / Coarse Sea Salt:** Provides critical mineral salinity to replace the missing sodium depth from aged hard cheeses .

---

## 3. Fresh Meats Counter
*Enforce the strict freshness clock. Request the freshest cuts directly from the counter.*

*   **Fresh Chicken Breasts / Thighs:** Non-aged, high-protein baseline structure; must be cooked immediately or flash-frozen at home .
*   **Fresh Ground Pork:** Rapid-turnover protein asset; ideal for quick pan-searing with zero cabinet-aging time .

---

## 4. Seafood Counter
*Avoid any fish sitting on open ice metrics that aren't verified flash-frozen.*

*   **Flash-Frozen-at-Sea White Fish (Cod or Halibut):** Caught and immediately frozen on the vessel, completely halting the bacterial breakdown of histidine into harmful histamines .

---

## 5. Dairy Case
*Strictly bypass the aged cheese caves. Limit selection to zero-aging, high-moisture items.*

*   **Whole Milk Ricotta:** Fresh, un-aged, high-moisture cheese providing a safe, luxurious fat asset without concentrated tyramine .
*   **Fresh Goat Cheese (Log or Crumbles):** Zero-cave-aging profile (e.g., Vermont Creamery); delivers a clean, tangy dairy finish for flatbread anchoring .

---

## 6. Hippie / Alternative / Vegan Section
*Isolate pure, single-ingredient assets devoid of industrial gums, seed oils, or synthetic phosphates.*

*   **Coconut Aminos:** An unfermented, low-amine alternative to traditional soy sauce; delivers a rich, sweet umami baseline .
*   **MALK Original Oat Milk or Elmhurst 1925 Unsweetened Oats:** Pure water-and-oat formulations entirely free from inflammatory rapeseed oils or synthetic inorganic phosphate buffers.
*   **Raw Pine Nuts:** Nutrient-dense edible seeds providing rich monounsaturated fats; delivers a buttery mouthfeel without true tree nut amine triggers .
*   **Raw Macadamia Nuts / Pure Macadamia Nut Butter:** Acts as a clean, high-fat cream emulsifier to successfully simulate the missing structural texture of parmesan cheese .

---

## 7. Juice & Beverage Cooler
*Avoid fermented tonics, kombuchas, or heavy citrus blends.*

*   **Pure Aloe Vera Juice (Inner Leaf Only):** Highly soothing to the mucosal lining of the GI tract; aids in reducing local enterocyte irritation .
*   **Premium Spring Water:** Clean, non-fluoridated hydration to assist kidneys with flushing out circulating systemic histamines.

---
